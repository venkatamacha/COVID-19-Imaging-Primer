COVID-19 pneumonia
Case contributed by Dr Maksym Kovratko
 Diagnosis certain 
Presentation
Fever, fatigue, dry cough and shortness of breath for 7 days. Traveled from Italy to Ukraine

Patient Data
AGE: 40 years
GENDER: Male
CT
CT
Axial non-contrast
CT images show multiple patchy, peripheral and basal, bilateral areas of ground-glass opacity. No mediastinal lymphadenopathy has been seen. 

Case Discussion
CT signs of a typical COVID-19 infection.

The RT-PCR COVID-19 test was positive.
