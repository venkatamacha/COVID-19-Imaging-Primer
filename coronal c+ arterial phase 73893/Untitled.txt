COVID-19 pneumonia
Case contributed by Dr Chong Keng Sang, Sam
 Diagnosis certain 
Presentation
Productive cough with a sore throat for 1 week, no fever or chest pain, traveling to Macau from Wuhan 3 days prior, denied close contact with wet market.

Patient Data
AGE: 50 years
GENDER: Female
X-RAY
Chest
X-ray
Multiple small bilateral areas of patchy confluent opacification, including a discrete rounded opacity in the right lower zone.

CT
Chest
CT
Coronal C+ arterial phase
Multiple cotton-like patches density images in peripheral of both lungs. No mediastinal, hilar or axillary lymphadenopathy.

Case Discussion
This patient tested positive for 2019-nCoV, and thus was deemed to have COVID-19 pneumonia.
